## FAQ Quick Answers

### Q: What data do you collect?
**A:** Almost nothing. We never collect, store, or see your screenshots, extracted text, or any screen content. Screenshots are deleted after 5 seconds.

### Q: Are screenshots stored anywhere?
**A:** No. Never. Screenshots exist in RAM for ~5 seconds, then permanently deleted. Never written to disk. Never recoverable.

### Q: Can you see my data?
**A:** No. We never see your screenshots or extracted text. Processing is fully automated. No human review. No manual access.

### Q: Is it more secure than email?
**A:** Yes. Email stores attachments permanently on servers. SnapTable deletes data instantly. Zero retention = zero risk.

### Q: HIPAA compliant?
**A:** Yes. HIPAA-eligible with BAA available. Zero data retention exceeds HIPAA requirements.

### Q: Used for AI training?
**A:** No. Never. We don't have your screenshots to train on - they're deleted immediately.
