Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

At SnapTable, privacy is not just a feature - it's the foundation of our service. We built SnapTable with a zero-retention architecture, meaning your data is never stored on our servers.

What We DON'T Collect

  • ✗ Your Screenshots - Deleted immediately after processing (within seconds)
  • ✗ Extracted Text/Data - Goes directly to your clipboard, we never see it
  • ✗ Usage Patterns - We don't track what you capture or when
  • ✗ AI Training Data - Your data is never used to train models

What We DO Collect

  • ✓ Account Information - Email address for account creation and login
  • ✓ Payment Information - Processed securely by Stripe (we never see your card details)
  • ✓ Usage Counts - Number of captures (not content) for subscription limits

Data Processing

When you capture a table with SnapTable:

  1. Your screenshot is encrypted and sent to our processing service
  2. Table extraction is performed automatically (no human review)
  3. Results are sent back to your clipboard
  4. The screenshot is immediately deleted from our systems

Total time on our servers: less than 5 seconds. No logging, no storage, no trace.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • ✓ TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • ✓ SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
  • ✓ HIPAA-eligible processing (BAA available for enterprise)

Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details. View Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✓ Access your account information
  • ✓ Delete your account at any time
  • ✓ Export your account data
