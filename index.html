Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does SnapTable support? +

SnapTable works with anything visible on your screen - PDFs, images (PNG, JPG), web pages, scanned documents, and more. If you can see it, you can capture it.

Is my data really private? +

Absolutely. SnapTable runs 100% on your computer. Your data never leaves your machine — nothing is uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere. We never see, collect, or analyze your data.

We DON'T collect:

  • ✗ Your information (deleted immediately)
  • ✗ Extracted text (goes to your clipboard only)
  • ✗ Personal information
  • ✗ Usage patterns or analytics
  • ✗ Your data for model training

Simple truth: We can't lose what we don't have.

Can anyone see my data? +

No. SnapTable runs entirely on your computer. Your screenshots and extracted data never leave your machine — not to us, not to any server, not to anyone. We literally cannot see your data.

Does it need internet? +

No. SnapTable works completely offline. It only needs internet once to activate your license key. After that, it works without any connection.

How accurate is the table extraction? +

SnapTable delivers high accuracy on standard tables, spreadsheets, PDFs, and web pages. It works best with clearly structured tables. Complex or poorly formatted tables may need minor touch-ups after pasting.

Can I use it on multiple computers? +

Your license key works on up to 2 computers — for example, your work PC and home PC. Need more? Contact us at support@snaptable.app.

Is it HIPAA / SOC 2 compliant? +

Yes. SnapTable runs entirely on your computer — data never leaves your machine. No data is transmitted, stored, or processed externally, making it ideal for regulated industries and sensitive data.
