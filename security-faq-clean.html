Are screenshots stored anywhere?
No. Never.

How it works:

  1. Screenshot captured to RAM only (~2-5 seconds)
  2. Sent encrypted to processing engine
  3. Table/text extracted and returned
  4. Screenshot permanently deleted
  5. Result copied to your clipboard

Total time: 2-5 seconds. Storage: Zero. Recovery: Impossible.

Your screenshots are:

  • ‚úó Never written to disk
  • ‚úó Never cached
  • ‚úó Never backed up
  • ‚úó Never logged
  • ‚úó Never recoverable (even by us)

  • Any content from your screen

Processing is fully automated. No human review. No manual access. Your data goes through encrypted systems and is deleted immediately.

Is my data encrypted?
Yes. Bank-level encryption everywhere.
